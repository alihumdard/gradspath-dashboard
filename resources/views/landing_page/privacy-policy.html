Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026. Grads Paths (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values transparency and user trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use the Grads Paths platform.

1. Platform Profiles & Visibility

Grads Paths allows mentors and contributors to create public-facing profiles so students can discover, evaluate, and book sessions. By creating a mentor account, you understand and agree that certain profile details may be visible to the public, including:

  • Your name or chosen display name
  • Educational background and credentials
  • Areas of expertise and offered services
  • Availability and session formats

You may edit, limit, or remove your profile at any time through your account settings. If you need assistance, you may contact us directly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Educational background or professional experience
  • Payment and payout information
  • Scheduling preferences and session details
  • Messages or communications sent through the platform

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser information
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the Grads Paths platform
  • Facilitate bookings, sessions, and group meetings
  • Process payments and payouts
  • Communicate account updates and service notices
  • Enforce platform policies and prevent misuse
  • Maintain platform safety, integrity, and quality

4. Sharing of Information

Grads Paths does not sell personal data.

We may share information only when necessary with:

  • Payment processors and scheduling providers
  • Technology and hosting partners
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, compliance)
  • Authorities when legally required

All third parties are expected to maintain appropriate confidentiality and security standards.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to safeguard your information, including:

  • Secure data storage and encrypted transactions
  • Limited internal access controls
  • Periodic security reviews

Despite these efforts, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Certain information may be retained where legally required or necessary for dispute resolution.

7. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tools to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Understand platform usage patterns
  • Improve performance and user experience

You may adjust cookie settings through your browser.

8. Age Requirements

Grads Paths is not intended for children under 13.

Users under 18 may require parental or guardian consent depending on applicable laws.

9. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
